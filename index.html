Flux NRW

Non-revenue water, NRW is the biggest challenge facing water utilities today. High volumes of water are lost through leaks, illegal connections, pilferage or simply customers being ‘under-invoiced’. FluxNRW is a specifically tailor-made NRW Management program customized for your Utility's specific needs. Through multi-phased systemic improvements for operational efficiency and technical mitigation, FluxNRW will establish how much water is lost, where and why the losses are occurring and provide data-driven strategies and mitigation to sustainably reduce your losses and drastically improve your revenue.
